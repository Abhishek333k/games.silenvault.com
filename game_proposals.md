# Game Concept Proposals Report
**Project:** Pegasus Game #1  
**Date:** May 15, 2026  
**Author:** Antigravity AI

## Introduction
This report outlines five simple yet visually premium game concepts designed for initial PC distribution with a roadmap for online cross-platform play.

---

## 1. Aetheria: The Sky Fragment
*   **Genre:** Atmospheric Puzzle Platformer
*   **Core Mechanic:** Low-gravity exploration and light-shard collection.
*   **Visual Identity:** Ethereal, Ghibli-esque floating islands with soft lighting.
*   **Online Potential:** High (Asynchronous multiplayer where players leave "light paths" for others).

## 2. Viper-Grid: Neon Strike
*   **Genre:** Synthwave Action-Arcade
*   **Core Mechanic:** High-speed grid movement with shooting and trail-management.
*   **Visual Identity:** Vibrant neon on deep black, high contrast, pulsing effects.
*   **Online Potential:** Very High (Real-time leaderboards and "Ghost" racing).

## 3. Mnemonic: The Digital Ghost
*   **Genre:** Cyberpunk Logic Puzzle
*   **Core Mechanic:** Rerouting data streams and solving logic-gate firewalls.
*   **Visual Identity:** Sleek terminal UI with glitch effects and holographic overlays.
*   **Online Potential:** Medium (Competitive hacking puzzles/speedruns).

## 4. Lumina: Deep Sea Odyssey
*   **Genre:** Survival / Exploration
*   **Core Mechanic:** Resource management (Oxygen/Light) in a dark abyss.
*   **Visual Identity:** Bioluminescent flora/fauna against deep oceanic blues.
*   **Online Potential:** Medium (Co-op exploration).

## 5. Bit-Beat: Rhythm Runner
*   **Genre:** Rhythm-based Auto-Runner
*   **Core Mechanic:** Syncing movement (jump/slide) to a procedural music beat.
*   **Visual Identity:** Minimalist pulsing geometry and high-speed motion effects.
*   **Online Potential:** High (Custom track sharing and global rhythm battles).
